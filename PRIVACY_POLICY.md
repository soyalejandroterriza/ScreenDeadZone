# Privacy Policy for ScreenDeadZone

*Last updated: June 30, 2026*

## English (EN)
**ScreenDeadZone** respects your privacy. This extension is designed to be 100% local and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or telemetry to any external servers.
- **Data Storage**: All user settings, including the dead zone size, layout mode, anchor position, and user-defined URLs (presets) are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **Network Requests**: The extension uses the `declarativeNetRequest` API solely to locally modify HTTP headers (`X-Frame-Options` and `Content-Security-Policy`) for the specific URLs you choose to embed in the extension's iframe. This is done entirely on your device to allow the content to render. No data is sent to the developer.
- **Third-Party Services**: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools.
